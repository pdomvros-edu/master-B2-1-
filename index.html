    Why B2 Level?

    Passing the B2 exam demonstrates an upper-intermediate command of English, which can help you:

    • Academic Pathways: Qualify for English-taught undergraduate programs and scholarships.
    • Professional Growth: Enhance your CV and open up job opportunities requiring solid English skills.
    • Everyday Confidence: Communicate effectively in travel, social, and informal work settings.
    • Global Mobility: Show readiness for long-term stays in English-speaking countries.
